Project goal

A simple daily loading plan from messy warehouse reality.

First-time loaders and ops teams lose time translating photos, labels, destinations, fragile rules, cold-chain rules, and truck dimensions into a sequence people can actually follow. DockMind compresses that work into one visual plan.

01 Detect cargo from photo or webcam 02 Fit boxes against truck constraints 03 Export a plan for humans and agents

Why it wins

It is not another chatbot. It is a visual operating surface.

The demo connects computer vision, constraint planning, 3D output, and role-specific agent instructions in one flow. Judges can see the truck, the exceptions, and the agent skill at the same time.
